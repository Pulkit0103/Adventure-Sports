ADVENTURE

Adventure Time

Adventure is a theme that has captivated the human imagination for centuries. It evokes feelings ofexictement, thrill, and discovery. Whether it's exploring a new place, trying a new activity, or pushing oneself to new limits, adventure is a powerful force that drives people to seek out new experiences.

Adventure can be found in many different forms, from extreme sports to travelling to new places, or even just stepping outside of one's comfort zone. Adventure is not only about the destination, but also about the journey and the lessons learned along the way.

BUNGEE JUMPING

Bungee jumping, also spelled bungy jumping, is an activity that involves a person jumping from a great height while connected to a large elastic cord. The launching pad is usually erected on a tall structure such as a building or crane, a bridge across a deep ravine, or on a natural geographic feature such as a cliff.

Bungee Jumping in India is for all those who can do a bit extra to pursue their passion. The craze of falling from a breathtaking altitude while tied with a rope sends chills down the spine.

PARAGLIDING

Paragliding is the recreational and competitive adventure sport of flying paragliders: lightweight, free-flying, foot-launched glider aircraft with no rigid primary structure. The pilot sits in a harness or lies prone in a cocoon-like 'pod' suspended below a fabric wing

Super-duper excited to fly like a bird? Try paragliding in India. Marvel at the majestic view of the country from a height you can only imagine. For daring souls, practicing some acrobats in the air is a must.

RAFTING

Rafting and whitewater rafting are recreational outdoor activities which use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on whitewater or different degrees of rough water. Dealing with risk is often a part of the experience.

India is blessed with many rivers. Despite owning religious significance, these gushing rivers pose a challenge to test your rafting skills. The time is right to invoke the rafter in you and go for river rafting in India. Making your way through grueling rapids is surely one of the best things to in India.
